Listing Options





View the Listing Options Below:


Basic Listing:
    A Basic Listing on Repoman.com is $100 per year per State or Province.
    By signing up for a Basic Listing you automatically receive a Free 30 Day Trial
    After your trial is complete, you will receive an email with information on how to continue your listing.
    Basic listings are the lowest ranked listings and will appear below companies that have submitted qualified insurance info or are enhanced listings
    There is no cost to add Logos that reflect your company's affiliations.
Basic Insured Listing:
    A Basic Insured Listing on Repoman.com is $100 per year per State or Province.
    By signing up for a Basic Listing you automatically receive a Free 30 Day Trial
    After your trial is complete, you will receive an email with information on how to continue your listing.
    Basic Insured listings are ranked higher than Basic Listings and display in a light gray box
    There is no cost to add Logos that reflect your company's affiliations.
Enhanced Listing:
    An Enhanced Listing on Repoman.com is $600 per year per State or Province.
    By signing up for an Enhanced Listing, you automatically receive a Free 30 Day Trial
    After your Trial is complete you will receive an email with information on how to continue your listing.
    Enhanced listings are ranked higher than Basic Listings and display in a red box.
    There is no cost to add Logos that reflect your company's affiliations.
Enhanced Listing w/ Multi-Media Ads:
    An Enhanced Listing w/ Multi-Media Ads on Repoman.com is $800 per year per State or Province.
    Signing up for an Enhanced Listing w/ Multi-Media Ads, you automatically receive a Free 30 Day Trial
    After your trial is complete you will receive an email with information on how to continue the pinnacle of your companies listing.
    Enhanced Listings w/ Multi-Media Ads have Maximum rank and display before enhanced and basic listings, as well as, being exhibited in a striking red container for proven visibility.
    Your company logo is broadcasted to thousands of users in the ad and serves as a go-to link to your company's website.
    Eye-catching links are displayed containing your company's detailed information packet and service coverage area map that encourage downloads, allowing clients access to the heart of your company.
    There is no cost to add Logos that reflect your company's affiliations.

Insurance Information:


Logos & Multi-Media ads are only an option for listings that have submitted insurance

There is no charge to have your ad indicate that you have submitted an insurance certificate

Insurance Certificate is to be furnished by Insurance Group/Agent with Repoman.com listed as certificate holder.
